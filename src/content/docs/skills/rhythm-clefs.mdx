Rhythm is the most common reason self-taught pianists stumble during reading. **By practicing rhythm separately from keyboard movement,** you isolate and strengthen your pulse without worrying about pitch or technique. Clef and note-decoding trainers, gathered here too, build the other half: naming any note on sight, away from the keys.
