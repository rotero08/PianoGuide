*Level Pre-Staff · the absolute start*

<Callout bordered={true} accentColor="gold" label="Your goal for this level" icon="target">
  Sit relaxed, find any key by feel without looking down, match a played pitch with your voice, and play simple five-finger patterns hands-together from directional (arrow) notation.
</Callout>

Before the staff appears, you build the two things everything else rests on: a relaxed body and a working ear. Reading at this stage is purely directional, just arrows showing up or down, so all of your attention can go to relaxation and keyboard geometry.
