Level 5: Early Polyphony

Your goal for this level

Read and play genuinely independent two-voice counterpoint, such as a Bach two-part invention, and run a spaced-repetition system that grinds down any passage that resists you.

You enter real classical polyphony. From here on, progress becomes an ongoing development loop powered by deliberate practice.

Technique the hands · hands-separate

Build true two-hand independence, and adopt a system that removes bottlenecks permanently.

    Manookian: Gradus, Vol. 1 / Bach 371Independent contrapuntal studies.

    Short contrapuntal studies, free on IMSLP, where each hand carries an independent line. Working them hands-separate and then together forces the left hand to stop merely following the right.

    Practice plan
    Hands-separate then together, daily. Done when the left hand leads its own line.
    Open resource ↗
    Anki spaced repetitionSpaced repetition for stuck passages.

    A free flashcard app built on spaced repetition, the scheduling method proven to maximise long-term retention. Make a deck called Piano Bottlenecks, cut a hard passage into a two-bar cell, and let the app resurface it on the optimal day.

    Practice plan
    Add two-bar cells; clear reviews daily. Done when the deck stays clear with no overdue cells.
    Open resource ↗
How the two halves connect hereThe principle is to read the whole and drill the cell. You read the invention hands-together to keep your eye-hand span growing, and whenever two bars resist, they go into Anki as a micro-cell and get solved hands-separate over spaced reviews, so the bottleneck never silently re-forms.

Practice rhythm

Daily · about 45 min

Fifteen minutes of contrapuntal hands-separate work, fifteen minutes of Anki bottleneck reviews, and fifteen minutes reading a new invention or Bartok passage.

Weekly

Add this week's two hardest bars to the Anki deck and clear everything overdue.

Monthly

Confirm independent voices and a working deliberate-practice loop.

  1. Read a two-part invention hands-together with both voices intact.
