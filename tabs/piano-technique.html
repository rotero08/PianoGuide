Biomechanics & Posture

Fluent reading is only possible with relaxed, tension-free movement. Tension is a major barrier to sight-reading: physical stress drains your focus and slows down your response.

Posture Guideline: Pain is a Warning

If you experience any physical pain, stop practicing immediately. Pain is a hard signal that something needs to change, whether it's your posture, playing position, or your need for rest.

No Teacher? Consider uploading a video of your hand posture to r/piano or r/pianolearning. The community can offer feedback on your technique, hand span, height, and body awareness (such as remembering to breathe while playing to avoid tension).

Alan Belkin: Arm Weight & Posture Video

A composer and longtime conservatory professor, Belkin explains the single most important physical idea for a beginner: let the natural weight of a relaxed arm fall into the key instead of pressing with the fingers. Watching this once and copying the loose, springy wrist drop prevents the stiff, finger-driven habit that causes most early tension and fatigue.
