Pre-Staff: Arm Weight & the Ear

Your goal for this level

Learn key placement, sing back pitches played on the piano, and follow five-finger layouts hands-together using directional (arrows) notation. No standard 5-line staff is used yet.

Before standard notation, you develop a secure physical foundation and ear connection. Reading at this stage is directional, using arrow movements so you can focus entirely on keyboard topology and arm drops.
